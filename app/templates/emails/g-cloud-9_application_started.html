Dear supplier,

You, or one of your colleagues, has started an application to offer your services through the G-Cloud 8 (G8) framework. All contributors on your Digital Marketplace account will now receive G8-related updates.

You can:
  • check your services are right for G8 (the scope is slightly different from G7):
    https://digitalmarketplace.blog.gov.uk/2016/05/16/g-cloud-8-redefining-the-specialist-cloud-services-category
  • find out what G8 means for you if you’re a G-Cloud 6 or G-Cloud 7 supplier:
    https://digitalmarketplace.blog.gov.uk/2016/04/18/what-g-cloud-8-means-for-g-cloud-6-and-g-cloud-7-suppliers
  • see the latest timetable of important dates:
    https://digitalmarketplace.blog.gov.uk/2016/05/17/g-cloud-8-apply-to-supply-your-cloud-services
  • find out how to apply:
    https://www.gov.uk/guidance/g-cloud-suppliers-guide#how-to-apply
  • find out when you can and when you can’t copy services in your G8 application:
    https://digitalmarketplace.blog.gov.uk/2016/05/11/copying-services-in-your-g-cloud-8-application
If you need to ask a question about G8, you must send it to the Crown Commercial Service (CCS) through your account on the Digital Marketplace before 5pm (BST) on 7 June 2016. We’ll post your question along with an answer from CCS in the updates page of your account:

https://www.digitalmarketplace.service.gov.uk/suppliers/frameworks/g-cloud-8/updates
